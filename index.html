Most freelancers and side hustlers calculate their hourly rate by dividing monthly income by hours worked. TrueRate accounts for all of it — giving you the number that actually lands in your bank account per hour.

How much tax do I pay on side hustle income in the UK?

In the 2025/26 tax year, UK self-employed income is subject to income tax and Class 4 National Insurance. You get a £1,000 trading allowance tax-free, and a personal allowance of £12,570 before income tax applies. Above that, the basic rate is 20% up to £50,270, then 40% on anything higher. Class 4 NI adds 6% on profits between £12,570 and £50,270. TrueRate applies all of these automatically based on your figures.
